Sir Francis Drake (Green)

In 1577 Queen Elizabeth I gave him money so that he could try to sail around the world.
He left with 5 ships including The Pelican, which later became known as The Golden Hind.
In 1588 Philip of Spain sent a large invasion fleet, The Armada, to attack the English.
Even though the English fleet was smaller, Drake used his knowledge to defeat the Armada and England was saved.
